Delicious Biryani


Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Meat of choice
  • Yogurt (curd)
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Ghee

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Fry onions (Birista)
  3. Cook rice (70% done)
  4. Cook Chicken
  5. Layering the biryani
  6. Dum Cooking
  7. Serve
Enjoy your homemade biryani!
